# Go-To-Market Strategy

## Target Customer
Startup CTOs, founders, and engineering managers at companies with 5-50 employees actively using multiple AI tools.

## Problem They Have
No visibility into total AI tool spend across the team. Individual subscriptions add up silently.

## Channels

### Primary
- Post on Hacker News "Show HN" — startup founders hang out there
- LinkedIn posts targeting startup CTOs and founders
- Cold email to YC alumni companies

### Secondary
- ProductHunt launch
- Reddit (r/startups, r/entrepreneur)
- Twitter/X fintech and startup communities

## Positioning
"Mint for AI tool spend" — familiar mental model, clear value prop.

## Launch Message
"Find out in 2 minutes if your team is overpaying for AI tools. Free audit, no signup."

## Conversion Funnel
Free audit → email capture → Credex consultation booking → paid engagement
