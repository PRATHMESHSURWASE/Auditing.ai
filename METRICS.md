# Metrics

## North Star Metric
Total monthly savings identified across all audits

## Acquisition Metrics
- Audits started per week
- Audits completed per week
- Completion rate (started vs finished)
- Traffic source breakdown

## Engagement Metrics
- Average tools per audit
- Average savings identified per audit
- Email capture rate (completed audits → email submitted)
- Share link clicks

## Conversion Metrics
- Consultation CTA click rate (for audits with savings > $500)
- Email → consultation booking rate

## Retention Metrics
- Return audit rate (same company runs audit again after changes)

## Success Benchmarks (Month 1)
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Audits completed | 100 |
| Avg savings identified | $400/mo |
| Email capture rate | 30% |
| Consultation bookings | 5 |
